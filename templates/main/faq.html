Order Tracking

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order by:

  1. Logging into your account and visiting "Order History"
  2. Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  3. Entering your tracking number on our website's tracking page
What are the shipping costs?

Our shipping options and costs:

  • Free Shipping: On all orders over $50
  • Standard Shipping: $5.99 (3-5 business days)
  • Express Shipping: $12.99 (1-2 business days)
  • Overnight Shipping: $24.99 (next business day)
How long will delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on shipping method and location:

Shipping Method Domestic International
Standard 3-5 business days 7-14 business days
Express 1-2 business days 3-5 business days
Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International orders may be subject to:

  • Customs duties and taxes (customer's responsibility)
  • Additional shipping fees
  • Longer delivery times

Check our international shipping page for country-specific information.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Order changes are possible if your order hasn't been processed for shipping:

  • Contact customer service immediately
  • Provide your order number and requested changes
  • Changes may result in price adjustments

Once shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and initiate a return if needed.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

Our return policy includes:

  • 30-day return window from delivery date
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Original tags and labels must be attached
  • Proof of purchase required

Some items are final sale (underwear, swimwear, personalized items).

How do I return an item?

Follow these steps to return an item:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "Order History"
  3. Select the item(s) to return
  4. Print the prepaid return label
  5. Package items securely
  6. Drop off at carrier location
When will I get my refund?

Refund processing times:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days after we receive return
  • PayPal: 1-3 business days
  • Store Credit: Immediately processed

Your bank may take additional 2-5 days to post the refund.

What if my item arrives damaged?

For damaged items:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of damaged item and packaging
  3. We'll arrange for replacement or refund
  4. Free return shipping for damaged items
Who pays for return shipping?

Return shipping costs depend on return reason:

  • Our mistake: Free return shipping
  • Damaged/Defective: Free return shipping
  • Wrong item sent: Free return shipping
  • Change of mind: Customer pays return shipping

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset link
  5. Create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

Is my payment information secure?

We take security seriously:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
  • We never store full credit card numbers
  • Tokenized payments for recurring charges
How do I update my account information?

To update your account:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Update your information
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Some changes may require verification for security purposes.

How is my personal data protected?

We protect your data by:

  • Complying with GDPR and CCPA regulations
  • Using encrypted databases
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data

Review our Privacy Policy for full details.

Do you offer two-factor authentication?

Yes, we offer 2FA for enhanced security:

  1. Go to "Account Security" in settings
  2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication
  3. Choose SMS or Authenticator app
  4. Follow setup instructions

We recommend enabling 2FA for all accounts.

Products & Inventory

Do you price match competitors?

Our price match policy:

  • We match prices from authorized retailers
  • Item must be identical (same model, color, size)
  • Must be in stock at competitor
  • Submit request within 14 days of purchase

Contact customer service with competitor's details.

How can I check item availability?

Check stock status by:

  1. Viewing product page for real-time inventory
  2. Signing up for back-in-stock notifications
  3. Contacting customer service

Inventory updates every 15 minutes.

What is your product quality guarantee?

We stand behind our products:

  • 100% satisfaction guarantee
  • Quality inspections before shipping
  • Manufacturer warranties apply
  • Extended warranties available for purchase
What warranties do you offer?

Warranty coverage includes:

  • Manufacturer Warranty: Varies by product (typically 1 year)
  • Extended Warranty: Available for electronics/appliances
  • Satisfaction Guarantee: 30 days for most items

Review product page for specific warranty details.

Where can I find product specifications?

Product details are available:

  1. On the product page under "Specifications"
  2. In downloadable PDF manuals
  3. By contacting our product specialists

We're happy to provide additional information upon request.

Payment Methods

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • QTipStore Gift Cards
  • Affirm financing (qualifying purchases)
Can I save multiple credit cards?

Yes, you can save payment methods:

  1. Go to "Payment Methods" in your account
  2. Click "Add New Payment Method"
  3. Enter card details
  4. Save for future purchases

You can set a default payment method for faster checkout.

How does PayPal checkout work?

Using PayPal:

  1. Select PayPal at checkout
  2. You'll be redirected to PayPal to log in
  3. Choose your PayPal funding source
  4. Confirm payment
  5. You'll return to our site to complete order
How do I use a gift card?

To redeem a gift card:

  1. Add items to your cart
  2. Proceed to checkout
  3. Enter gift card code in "Payment Method" section
  4. Apply to your order

Gift cards never expire and can be combined with other payment methods.

Do you offer installment plans?

We offer installment options through Affirm:

  • Available for purchases over $50
  • 3, 6, or 12 month payment plans
  • Soft credit check required
  • Select at checkout

Interest rates vary based on creditworthiness.
