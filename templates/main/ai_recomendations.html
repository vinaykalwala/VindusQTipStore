How It Works

Our AI system analyzes various data points to create a unique profile for you:

  • Browsing Behavior: Pages you view, time spent, and search terms used.
  • Purchase History: Items you've bought and how frequently.
  • Wishlist & Favorites: Products you’ve saved for later.
  • Interaction Patterns: Products you click, add to cart, or remove.
  • Category Interest: Frequent visits to specific product categories.
  • Similar User Profiles: Recommendations based on customers with similar tastes.

By combining these insights, we deliver intelligent, dynamic product suggestions that evolve with your needs.
